2019 VAR have the copyright/'published as the paper Sport Automation Finished Abstract—Today’s game quality is more affected by referee decisions. The history shows that the error in a referee’s decision may change the whole result of a game. It seems to
rely on just the referee’s decision and always combines with the errors. This fact is even bolded in important games like the World Cup or Olympics which may cause the rank of the team, players, etc. Recently, to solve this problem, the new concept of VAR (video assistant referee) has been introduced and had also been hired during the football World Cup match 2018. Even some methods like video check-ins in volleyball games or bypass signal in Swordsmanship (sword fighting) long back was used. But still, so many demand games are missed. Statistically, almost approximately 8 hundred million people are playing table tennis in the world but due to the ball speed in this game which sometimes increases up to 69.9 miles per hour (112.4931 km/h) which brings the limitation to hire and use the VAR systems. In this research paper, a novel VAR system (TTV system) based on Kinect and Raspberry pi has been presented. The main aspect of this design is to have a portable system which can be used for any type of LCDsystem and help the referees to count the ball better during the game to have a more qualified game. In the proposed TTV algorithm, the two methods of contour and depth are merged to have better ball detection. The experiment results in showing the proposed algorithm and systems success in real game and its ability to implement in the real game which has not been reported before.		 https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1jU4y137PH
PI Scada published as the paper : Ata Jahangir Moshayedi,Atanu Shuvam Roy,Lifa Liao,Shuai Li, Raspberry Pi SCADA Zonal based System for Agricultural Plant Monitoring SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) and DCS (Distributed Control System) are both famous terms in automation and they both have critical rules. There is rarely an industry in the world which are not auditing their outcome based on one of this terms. But IOT developed in recent years, mixing the functionality of these two terms has been going on too with various security challenges involved. Even some previous issues like the price of software and easy extendibility of them make the way for small industries to have their benefits. In this research paper along with the designing and comparison, two small SCADA systems for agricultural applications tries to reduce the size as well as address a zonal method to investigate the security problems. Till these days’ researchers have shown lots of development to reduce the scada cost in different manner but, having the compact, powerful and small size host for SCADA system has not been reported before. One of the novel aspect in this design is hiring the Raspberry Pi as the smallest computer instead of PC or laptop which along with reducing the system cost can also reduce the whole design system. Beside, Web based SCADA system has been successfully designed here and used. This paper discusses the context of local as well as remote monitoring of an agricultural plant environment with the help of a Raspberry Pi network through which a SCADA System was established. This paper aims to prove that a far more efficient and cost-effective along a secure SCADA System for monitoring is possible with the lowest of efforts. So, having zonal idea will help system to achieve the aim of secure sensor
monitoring without any kind of outside interference. The result shows the successful design and implementation of zonal idea as well as the perfect usage of Raspberry Pi as a small andreliable host.

DSS（Diagnostic Smart System）		 have the copyright webservice and App for Automation Finished CRM is one of the key point of any product these days. Many products fail because of lack of CRM. The key of CRM is the relationship between customer and manufacturer. It means that one customer needs good service and the other manufacturer needs more celling products. In the other word, CRM is a business strategy to select valuable customers and manage the relationships with them. Its goal is to reduce sales cycle and cost, increase revenue and find new markets and channels to expand business by improving customer value, satisfaction, profitability and loyalty. On the other hand, study on the products shows, in terms of performance and working state, product performance sometimes fails due to some working conditions which are not considered and observed by the designer or may not be considered due to the reason of material site. Also testing the product in all environment is not possible and over than time it may be very expensive for some products. Based on knowing the importance of CRM concept; DSS is a system which try to connect the rings between manufacture from one side and customer from other side in an intelligent way. Many manufacturers to make their working cycle simpler tried to hand over their support system to customer service section and it may have happened the data from customer service which may include the product Diffraction, Fault and any error type will not reach the manufacture for study and rectification.

2018 ISR Applied for patent Phase 1 Finished At present, our society increasing aging population, labor service costs, labor supply and demand and there is a huge demand for labor; users increasingly emphasize fresh, thoughtful consumer experience, businesses pursuit of low cost and high efficiency of the purpose of profit, which service robot development and application to create a broad market prospect.The development of service robots contending products to Alibaba, represented by Ma Fresh green box service robots is to enter the 2018 First China International Import Expo exhibition site, it is to see the style. At present, the development of service robots following defects:
· Single function: only for a specific environment to achieve specific functions, has a tendency toys;
· Not flexible enough: You can not use the flexible robotic arm, unable to move, the need for orbit;
· Unable Collaboration: robot exists only as an individual, no scheduling between multiple robots
To solve these problems, the project team proposed a "service robot integration and collaborative platform" solution, including:
· According to insufficient research and development of service robots designed "highly integrated" robot, which set multiple functions in one of the robot;
· Even if the robot should be more integrated functionality on demand, a combination of functional modules designed "low coupling", to facilitate the combination of different functions and modules, and dismantling;
· To overcome the lack of independent operation of a conventional service robots, designed the "synergy" type of robot system scheduling, task more robots will be "total station" unified scheduling and distribution;
The project is to overcome the traditional single function service robots, less flexible, more individual robot can not be coordinated scheduling problem and design a highly integrated, low coupling, can be scheduled cooperative multi-robot scenes and service platform, can be put into restaurants, hotels, bar, bath center, snack shop and a hospital nurse stations, wards and nursing homes and other service establishments. The product has been put into use Jiangxi University canteen, national invention patent applications related to the results of nine published papers related to eight.
